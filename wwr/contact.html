HISTORY

Company Name: Wireframe Solutions Founded: 2010 Founders: John Lee and Emily Chen Early Days: Wireframe Solutions was founded by John Lee and Emily Chen, two passionate designers who met while working at a digital agency. They saw an opportunity to specialize in wireframing and user experience design, and started the company with a small team of three people. Growth: The company quickly gained traction, and by 2012, they had grown to a team of 10 people. They started working with major clients in the tech industry and expanded their services to include prototyping and usability testing. Milestones: - 2015: Wireframe Solutions opened a second office in New York City. - 2017: The company launched its own design tool, WireframePro, which became a popular choice among designers. - 2019: Wireframe Solutions was acquired by a leading design firm, expanding its global reach. Today: Wireframe Solutions is a leading wireframing and user experience design company, with a team of over 50 people and offices in three cities. They continue to innovate and push the boundaries of design, helping clients create user-centered products and experiences.
