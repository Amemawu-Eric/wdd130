Overview

purpose

1. Increase visibility: Reach a wider audience, including potential customers who are searching for rafting adventures online. 2. Build credibility: Showcase our expertise, safety record, and customer testimonials to establish trust with potential customers. 3. Provide information: Share details about our services, including trip options, pricing, and what to expect. 4. Bookings and reservations: Allow customers to easily book and pay for trips online. 5. Marketing and promotion: Share updates, special offers, and news about our business to attract new customers and keep existing ones engaged. 6. Compete with other outfitters: Establish a strong online presence to stay competitive in the market. 7. Gather feedback: Collect customer reviews and feedback to improve our services and customer experience. 8. Sell merchandise: Offer branded merchandise, such as t-shirts, hats, and water bottles, to customers and enthusiasts.

Audience Who are the target customers? - Demographics: Age 25-50, both individuals and families - Interests: Outdoor enthusiasts, adventure-seekers, nature lovers, and those looking for a unique experience - Financial situation: Middle to upper-middle-class individuals and families with a disposable income for recreational activities Describe their life (or business) situation - Active lifestyle, enjoy trying new experiences, and value quality time with family and friends - possibly stressed or overwhelmed with work and daily life, seeking a break and a chance to disconnect - interested in exploring new outdoor activities and destinations What do they want? - A fun and exciting experience for themselves or their family - A safe and guided adventure with experienced instructors - Information on available rafting trips, prices, and packages - Visuals and testimonials to help them decide and get excited about the experience What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website? - Clear information on trip options, prices, and packages - Answers to questions like "What to expect?", "What to bring?", and "Is it safe?" - Visuals like photos and videos to showcase the experience - Testimonials and reviews from previous customers - Easy online booking and payment options What are their needs that aren't being met? - A user-friendly website that provides all the necessary information and answers their questions - A clear and easy booking process - More visuals and testimonials to help them feel confident in their decision - A sense of community and connection with other outdoor enthusiasts How will my audience usually access my site? - Desktop and laptop computers for research and booking - Mobile devices for quick checks and inspiration - Social media platforms for community engagement and inspiration - Search engines like Google for searching specific keywords like "water rafting near me" or "whitewater rafting trips"
