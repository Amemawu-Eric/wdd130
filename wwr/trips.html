Whitewater Rafting on the Zambezi River

Whitewater Rafting on the Zambezi River* Brace yourself for the ultimate rafting experience on the mighty Zambezi River. Tackle the world-renowned rapids beneath the majestic Victoria Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World. *Highlights*: Grade V rapids, breathtaking scenery, Victoria Falls visit.

Historic Journey Through Egypt

Historic Journey Through Egypt Step back in time as you explore the ancient wonders of Egypt. Visit the Great Pyramids, sail down the Nile, and uncover the mysteries of the pharaohs in the Valley of the Kings. *Highlights*: Pyramids of Giza, Nile cruise, Luxor Temple.

Great Barrier Reef Diving Adventure*

Dive into the underwater paradise of the Great Barrier Reef. Discover vibrant coral gardens, encounter exotic marine life, and immerse yourself in one of the most diverse ecosystems on the planet. *Highlights*: Scuba diving, snorkeling, marine conservation.

Culinary Tour Through Tuscany

Savor the flavors of Italy on a culinary tour through Tuscany. Indulge in wine tastings, cooking classes, and visits to local markets, all while enjoying the rolling hills and historic charm of the Tuscan countryside. *Highlights*: Wine tasting, cooking classes, truffle hunting.
